OUR SERVICES

Custom Homes

Established in 1981, Arrowhead Homes is committed to building each Custom Home as if it were our own. Our high standards and attention to detail exceed our competitors. Your new home will have the right mix of style and quality with the craftsmanship you expect. There is no middle man. Lynn invests time into understanding our client’s vision prior to commencing any custom home.

In addition to building the most beautiful custom homes in the Mid-Michigan area, we're serious about using the best and longest lasting materials possible. That's why we're proud to offer Superior Walls with our builds. Superior Walls are made here in Michigan, last longer and more durable than a traditional foundation wall. Home Remodeling

Arrowhead Homes works with a devoted team of Sub-Contractors, many of whom have been on our team for over 25 years. Our passion for building includes restoring and renovating homes. Whether you are intend to finish a lower level, remodel a kitchen, build a deck or a new garage; Arrowhead Homes team of Contractors is prepared to carry out and manage your project to completion.

Land Development

Do You Have Your Own Property?
Great! As a custom builder & land developer, Lynn knows that each lot has unique characteristics. We will build your home on your land – the way you want it. By using your plan or customizing one of ours; we will work with you to make sure your home fits perfectly.

Free Home Site Evaluation:
We would be happy to come out and do a free home site evaluation. Lynn will meet with you on the site and answer questions like: how your home will be positioned on the lot, what trees need to be cleared or what utilities are available.

Are You Looking For Property?
That’s no problem. Most Arrowhead Homes clients don’t have land before they meet with us. We have many lots from which to choose. We are also members of the Greater Lansing Association of Realtors. Access to the Multiple Listing Service will assist in finding your perfect lot.
