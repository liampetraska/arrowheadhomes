Made and manufactured near Holland, MI, Superior Walls are just that - a superior choice for your foundation. We believe in their quality so much that Arrowhead Homes is a Premier Featured Builder for Superior Walls in the Mid-Michigan area with 15 years experience of installing these walls. Below are just a few of the benefits to Superior Walls over traditional foundation walls.

15 Year Warranty

Knowing that your home is built on a strong foundation is one of the most basic and important steps to building any home. That's why Superior Walls stands by their product with a 15 year limited warranty. That's 5 years longer than traditional foundation walls!

Better Insulation and Climate Endurance

Superior Walls are an XI wall that come standard R15 insulation. That's right - they come from the factory with a higher insulation value than required! In addition to better insulation, these walls are not affected by climate changes like traditional walls. If you live in Michigan, you know how quickly the weather can change. Knowing your foundation can handle all that Michigan can throw at it goes a long way.
