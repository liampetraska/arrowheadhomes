Custom Homes

Founded in 1981 by L. Lynn Ball, Arrowhead Homes was created out of a passion to deliver efficient, high quality expertly crafted homes. Your participation in the design is as important as the homes construction. Flexibility allows you, the customer to make changes throughout the building process. Your “partnership” with Lynn allows for personal input from conception to completion. This atmosphere creates a relationship that is built on confidence and satisfaction.
